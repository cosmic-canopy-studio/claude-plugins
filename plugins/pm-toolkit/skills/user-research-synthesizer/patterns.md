# User Research Synthesizer - Detailed Patterns

## Input Types

- Interview transcripts
- Survey responses
- Customer feedback logs
- Support ticket analysis
- User session recordings notes
- NPS/CSAT comments

## Output Template

```markdown
# User Insights: [Topic]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Researcher:** [Name]
**Data Sources:** [List of sources analyzed]
**Sample Size:** [N interviews/responses]

---

## Executive Summary

[2-3 sentences: key insight, primary need identified, main recommendation]

---

## User Segments Observed

### Segment 1: [Segment Name]

**Characteristics:**
- [Defining characteristic]
- [Behavior pattern]

**Observed in:** [N] of [Total] participants

---

## Behavioral Patterns

### Pattern 1: [Pattern Name]

**Description:** [What users do]

**Evidence:**
- "[Quote from user]" - Participant [N]
- "[Quote from user]" - Participant [N]
- Observed in [N] of [Total] participants

**Implication:** [What this means for product]

---

## Pain Points

### Pain Point 1: [Title]

**Description:** [The problem users face]

**Severity:** High | Medium | Low

**Evidence:**
- "[Quote]" - Participant [N]
- [Specific observation]

**Frequency:** [N] of [Total] mentioned this

---

## User Needs

### Primary Need: [Need]

**Description:** [What users need]

**Evidence:** [Supporting quotes/observations]

### Secondary Needs

1. [Need 1] - Evidence: [Quote/observation]
2. [Need 2] - Evidence: [Quote/observation]

---

## Key Quotes

| Quote | Participant | Context |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| "[Quote]" | P1 | [Context] |
| "[Quote]" | P2 | [Context] |

---

## Recommendations

1. **[Recommendation]**
   - Based on: [Pattern/Pain Point]
   - Impact: High | Medium | Low

---

## Gaps in Research

- [What we still don't know]
- [Areas needing more research]

---

## Methodology

**Research method:** [Interviews, surveys, etc.]
**Sample:** [Description of participants]
**Duration:** [When research was conducted]
**Limitations:** [Known limitations]
```

## Quality Rules

**NEVER:**
- Fabricate statistics ("68% of users...")
- Make unsupported generalizations
- Extrapolate beyond data
- Claim certainty without evidence

**ALWAYS:**
- Cite specific sources
- Include verbatim quotes
- Note sample size limitations
- Mark inferences as hypotheses
