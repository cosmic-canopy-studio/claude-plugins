# Market Analyzer

## Quick Start

| You Say | Result |
|---------|--------|
| "What's the market size for X?" | TAM/SAM/SOM with sources |
| "What are the trends in this space?" | Trend analysis with evidence |
| "Is this market growing?" | Growth analysis with drivers |

## Announcement
"I'm using the market-analyzer to research [market/segment]..."

## Process
1. Gather market data from provided sources
2. Calculate TAM/SAM/SOM with methodology
3. Identify growth drivers and constraints
4. Analyze adoption patterns
5. Save to `research/market-YYYY-MM-DD-{market}.md`

## Core Principle
**Cite all quantitative claims.** Note data freshness and confidence levels.

## Output
Market analysis with:
- Executive summary (size + growth + opportunity)
- TAM/SAM/SOM with calculations
- Growth projections with CAGR
- Market trends with evidence
- Adoption patterns and barriers
- Key players and market share

## Quality Rules
- Cite all quantitative claims
- Note data freshness and reliability
- Mark projections vs. historical data
- Include confidence levels

## Transitions
- **Before**: User asks about market opportunity or sizing
- **After**: competitive-analyzer (player analysis) or prd-generator (feature specs)

See [patterns.md](patterns.md) for output template and detailed workflow.
